Welcome to Privacy at Chef+ (“we”). In this policy, we outline what data we collect and how we use it.



Personal Information



When you create an account on Chef+, you are asked to enter personal data. The type of data collected depends on the type of sign up options you chose, that’s user account creation, artist account creation or anonymous. In both cases, this may include: profile name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender, country, region, street address, government identification.


Service


To make Chef+ work, we collect information about how you use our service. Examples include: search queries, taste preferences, collections you create, browsing history, account settings. We use data to enhance and personalize our service. For example, we use such information to: provide localized content, provide you with personalized recommendations for recipes and videos we think will be of interest to you.
